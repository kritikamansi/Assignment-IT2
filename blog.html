RISE IN TECHNOLOGY

With the arrival of the twenty-first century, the globe witnessed the birth of a new era in which people and machines coexisted to accelerate the growth of both. The transformation was seen all across the world as each country strove to push limits, uncover breakthroughs, and discover technologies to improve the lives of the people who live here, at the very least, a little bit better. However, as technology advances and finds new methods to alleviate the difficulties of daily living, it also discovers new ways to take life. Warfare today is conducted on the same principles that prior battles were fought on: greater weaponry, tactics, and numbers created by the technology of the period.It's as easy as that: whomever is more advanced wins. This persists in people's thoughts today, with individuals seeking to outdo one other in terms of gadgetry and so-called 'innovation.' Technology, at times resembling paradise or hell, may one day bring humans to one of these destinations. People now regard technology to be the devices that are easily available to them; their cellphones, tablets, computers, and so on. It has become a standard.
